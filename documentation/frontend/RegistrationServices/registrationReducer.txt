The RegistrationReducer contains all business logic for registration related operaiton

=====================================================================

uploadInviteStudentsCSV: upload a csv file for student registration

uploadInviteFacultyCSV: upload a csv file for faculty registration

uploadAddCoursesCSV: upload a csv file for adding courses

uploadEnrollStudentsCSV: upload a csv to enroll students to courses

uploadEnrollInstructorsCSV: upload a csv to enroll instructors to courses
